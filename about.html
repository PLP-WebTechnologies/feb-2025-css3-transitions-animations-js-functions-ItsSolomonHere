About BiKEiT

BiKEiT is your premier online destination for discovering and purchasing high-performance superbikes and rugged adventure motorcycles. We are passionate about providing Kenyan riders with access to the best brands in the industry, including KTM, BMW, Suzuki, CFMoto, TVS, Bajaj, Honda, and more.

Our mission is to empower your riding experience by offering a curated selection of bikes, competitive prices in Kenyan Shillings (KSh), and a user-friendly online platform. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a speed enthusiast, BiKEiT has the perfect motorcycle for you.

We are committed to excellent customer service and building a community of passionate riders. Contact us today to find the bike of your dreams!
